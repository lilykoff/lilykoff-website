In summer 2019 and summer 2020, I worked for US Ski and Snowboard as a high performance intern and Women in Sports Technology (WiST) Fellow; my research projects included visualizing force plate output, using machine learning to predicting performance from training data, and modeling medal probabilities to inform team selection criteria.
